Welcome to Pangat Restaurant

Experience the authentic flavors of traditional Indian cuisine at Pangat Restaurant. Our journey began with a passion for serving wholesome, flavorful meals that bring families together and create lasting memories.

Every dish is prepared with love, using time-honored recipes passed down through generations. From our signature Maharashtrian specialties to popular North Indian delicacies, we ensure each meal is a celebration of taste and tradition.
